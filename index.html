New York Hacker Society

aut viam inveniam aut faciam

What?

A regular gathering of interesting, friendly people who like to make things.

Why?

  • There are a lot of smart, creative people in the New York startup community.
  • We don’t see each other often enough.
  • It would be much easier if we formed a regular gathering.

When & Where?

  • We meet every other Wednesday at 7:00pm.
  • It started on January 21st, 2015.
  • For now, we meet at Scratcher. We’re open to better ideas.
